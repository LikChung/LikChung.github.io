“Everybody has a forest of his own. Those lost have lost and those have met before will meet each other again.”     ― Haruki Murakami, Norwegian Wood

Hi, I’m Jerry Chung

A PhD student pursuing chemistry in Hong Kong, specializing in trace analysis in the environmental system.

Despite keeping a black dog, I am still enthusiastic about life.

I’m an avid reader (a big Murakami fan), but I also love to organize and export.

Besides books, I’m keen on music (especially Indie, City Pop, and Hip-Jazz) and the 1990’s Japanese anime.

Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact me!

Glad to meet you at IForester

IForester is my personal blog to keep track of my life and notes of interest to me.

The name is taken from Haruki Murakami’s 1987 novel, Norwegian Wood.

Coz I deeply believe that people meet and separate from each other like a game of hide and seek in the forest.
